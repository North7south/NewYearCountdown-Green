Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

Summary: New Year Countdown Timer does not collect, store, transmit, or process any user data. This extension operates entirely locally in your browser.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the New Year Countdown Timer Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information Collection

We do NOT collect any information. The Extension does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Store cookies or local storage data
  • Access your browsing history
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Request or require any permissions

3. How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely within your local browser environment. It:

  • Calculates the countdown to New Year 2027 using your device's system time
  • Displays the countdown with a visual interface and animations
  • Runs all calculations client-side (in your browser)
  • Does not communicate with any external servers

4. Permissions

This Extension requires zero permissions. It does not need access to:

  • Your tabs or browsing activity
  • Your personal data
  • External websites
  • Storage or file systems

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with, connect to, or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

6. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security concerns related to personal information. The Extension operates in a completely isolated manner within your browser.

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The Extension is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Open Source

This Extension's code is open source and available for review. You can verify our privacy claims by examining the source code at any time.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete privacy when using this Extension.
